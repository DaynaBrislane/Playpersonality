Dimension Deep Dive

Energy and Communication

The Highlights
  • Energized by people and collaboration
  • Thinks out loud and brainstorms freely
  • Initiates conversations easily
  • Thrives in group settings
  • Needs alone time to recharge occasionally
How you show up with others

You draw energy from being around people. Social interactions fuel your creativity and motivation, and you naturally gravitate toward collaborative environments.

  • Prefer talking through ideas rather than working in isolation
  • Often the one to break the ice or rally a group
  • Can sometimes overwhelm quieter colleagues with enthusiasm

Information Processing

The Highlights
  • Big-picture first
  • Idea-led and imaginative
  • Pattern-seeking
  • Experimental and Original
  • Balanced enough to ground ideas when needed
How you make sense of information

You tend to grasp the essence of things quickly, often before all the details are on the table.

  • Look for patterns, meaning, and the "why"
  • Take in information in snapshots, not step-by-step instructions
  • Handle facts well, just not first

Decision Making

The Highlights
  • Leads with empathy and values
  • Considers impact on people first
  • Seeks harmony in team dynamics
  • Motivated by authenticity
  • Can weigh logic when stakes are high
How you make decisions

Your decisions are deeply influenced by how they affect the people around you. You prioritize alignment with personal values and team wellbeing over pure efficiency.

  • Naturally attuned to how others feel about a direction
  • May delay tough calls to avoid conflict or hurt feelings
  • Strongest when pairing instinct with data to back it up

Organization

The Highlights
  • Flexible and spontaneous
  • Keeps options open as long as possible
  • Adapts quickly to changing plans
  • Creative under pressure
  • Can build structure when the project demands it
How you organize your work

You prefer to stay open and exploratory rather than locked into rigid plans. Deadlines can be motivating, but you do your best work when there's room to pivot and iterate.

  • Thrive with flexibility but can struggle with long-term follow-through
  • Often juggle multiple projects and ideas at once
  • Benefit from lightweight systems that keep you on track without feeling constrained

DoBring 2–3 options, not an open question. She'll commit confidently with a frame
AvoidOpen-ended "what do you think we should do?" — it can feel like the burden is shifted
