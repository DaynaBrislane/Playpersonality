How you work best

Based on Nadia’s context. You can edit anything that doesn’t sound like you.

What energizes me

I get energy from people, ideas, possibility, and meaningful work that connects to a real human impact. I’m at my best when we’re building momentum together and there’s room to be creative.

DoBring energy and possibility, because I light up around forward motion.
AvoidReducing work to routine tasks, because monotony drains my focus quickly.

How to help me decide

I make decisions through both possibility and people impact. I care about what works, but I also care deeply about how the decision lands for the team.

DoShow the people impact, because that heavily shapes my judgment.
AvoidFraming decisions as purely logical, because context and feelings matter to me.

How to schedule with me

I like flexibility and room to adapt, and I can resist overly rigid planning. I also know I can overcommit when something feels exciting, so clear boundaries help me.

DoOffer options with clear purpose, because I value choice and context.
AvoidPacking my calendar tightly, because I need breathing room to stay present.

How to bring me information

I naturally look for patterns, meaning, and the bigger picture before I zoom into details. I can handle facts and specifics, but I need to understand why they matter first.

DoLead with the big picture, because I connect details through purpose.
AvoidStarting with dense minutiae, because I may lose the thread early.
